KVKL Official Scorecard









Mark Male (M) Female (F) or Non-Binary (NB) for all players and circle to denote captains.

"Line-up Rule" (Rule C.4): Kicking lineups must contain between 8 and 12 spots. No lineup may include more than: (a) 8 spots containing men or 8 spots containing women, if the lineup contains 11 or 12 spots; (b) 7 spots containing men or 7 spots containing women, if the lineup contains 10 spots; (c) 6 spots containing men or 6 spots containing women, if the lineup contains 9 spots; (d) 5 spots containing men or 5 spots containing women, if the lineup contains 8 spots. Two players may share one spot in the kicking order as long as: (a) Both players kick and field at least once during the game. (b) The lineup already contains 10 kicking spots. (c) The minimum designations in Rule C.4 are met. (d) Any player who kicks out of order shall be called a dead-ball out.
