"Line-up Rule" (Rule C.4): Kicking orders shall contain between 8 to 12 spots. Kicking orders with 11 or 12 spots must designate no more than 8 spots to a gender, orders with 10 spots no more than 7 to a gender, orders with 9 spots no more than 6 to a gender, and orders with 8 spots no more than 5 to a gender. 2 players may share a single spot in the order as long as both players kick at least once in the game and the lineup contains at least 10 spots.
